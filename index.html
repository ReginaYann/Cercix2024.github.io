Dataset Description

Cervix2024 is an in-house dataset that includes multi-series cervical MRI images, which is collected from PLA General Hospital(301 Hospital).

We are excited to offer our open-source dataset to researchers and practitioners in the field. If you are interested in using this dataset for your projects or research, please follow the steps below to apply for access: Application Form: Complete the Dataset Access Application Form with your contact information, affiliation, and a brief description of your intended use of the dataset. Terms of Use: Review and agree to the Terms of Use for our dataset. This includes acknowledgment of data privacy, ethical use, and citation requirements. Approval Process: Submit your application form for review. Our team will evaluate your request and respond within 5-7 business days. Data Access: Once approved, you will receive an email with instructions on how to securely access and download the dataset. Please ensure that you follow all guidelines and regulations as stated in the Terms of Use. We look forward to supporting your research and innovation.
